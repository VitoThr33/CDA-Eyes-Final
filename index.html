about us

true eyecare for your family

Dr. Kolten R. Kuntz a fourth generation optometrist, Dr. Koten Kuntz attended Eastern Washington University and recieved his Bachelor of Science in Biology in 2008. He received his Doctor of Optometry degree in 2014 from the Arizona College of Optometry. During his fourth your of optometric eduacation he acquired additional training for the diagnosis and treatment of Dry Eye Disease through externships at the Schwartz Laser Eye Center and Horizon laser Vision Center located in Scottsdale, AZ.

Dr. Kuntz went on to further his education by completing a one year, post-graduate residency in ocular disease, contact lenses, and low vision services through Pacific University at the Spokane Veterans Affairs Medical Center.

Dr. Kuntz provides comprehensive primary eye care to patients of all ages, treatement of ocular disease, and year of experience diagnosing and treating chronic dry eye. He is enthusiastic about providing quality care to the Willamette Valley communities. In his spare time Dr. Kuntz and his wife Haley enjoy spending time outdoors and exploring the beautiful Pacific Northwest. He enjoys caring for others and being involved within the community
